<p><b><i>There&nbsp;are&nbsp;no changes to the advisory level or risk indicators. Advisory summary was updated.<br />
<br />
</i>Exercise normal precautions&nbsp;in&nbsp;Barbados.</b></p>
<p>Barbados is&nbsp;generally a&nbsp;safe destination for travelers.</p>
<ul>
<li>Petty crime is common, especially in popular tourist locations. Stay aware of your surroundings.</li>
<li>Most crimes against foreigners are crimes of opportunity, like purse snatching and pickpocketing.</li>
</ul>
<p>Review our&nbsp;travel guidance for&nbsp;Barbados&nbsp;to learn how to prepare for a safe trip.</p>
<h3>If you decide to travel to&nbsp;Barbados:&nbsp;</h3>
<ul>
<li>Enroll in the&nbsp;<a href="https://mytravel.state.gov/s/step" title="Smart Traveler Enrollment Program (STEP)">Smart Traveler Enrollment Program (STEP)</a>&nbsp;to get important updates and alerts from the U.S. embassy or consulate. Enrolling helps the U.S. embassy or consulate contact you or your emergency contact in an emergency.&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Do not physically resist any robbery attempt.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Use caution when walking or driving at night.</li>
<li>Keep a low profile.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Stay aware of your surroundings.</li>
<li>Do not display signs of wealth, like expensive watches or jewelry.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Do not answer your door at your hotel or residence unless you know who it is.</li>
<li>Stay alert in tourist spots.&nbsp;&nbsp;</li>
<li>Stay alert when visiting banks or ATMs.&nbsp;</li>
<li>Review the&nbsp;<a href="https://www.osac.gov/Country/Barbados/Detail" target="_blank" title="Country Security Report">Country Security Report</a>&nbsp;for Barbados.</li>
<li>Visit the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC) website for the&nbsp;<a href="https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/destinations/traveler/none/barbados" target="_blank" title="CDC Travel Health Information">latest&#8239;Travel Health Information&#8239;</a>for&nbsp;Barbados.</li>
<li>Whether&nbsp;you&#8217;re&nbsp;a first time or frequent traveler, use the&nbsp;<a href="https://travel.state.gov/en/international-travel/planning/checklist.html" title="International Travel Checklist">International Travel Checklist</a>.</li>
<li>We highly recommend that you buy travel insurance before you travel.&#8239;Check with&nbsp;your&#8239;<a href="https://travel.state.gov/en/international-travel/planning/guidance/insurance.html" title="Travel Insurance">travel insurance provider</a>&#8239;about evacuation&nbsp;assistance, medical insurance, and trip cancelation coverage.</li>
</ul>
