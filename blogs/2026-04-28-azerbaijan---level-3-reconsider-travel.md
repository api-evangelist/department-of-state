<p><b><i>There was no change to the advisory level or risk indicators. Advisory summary and areas of increased risk were updated.</i></b></p>
<p>Reconsider Travel to <b>Azerbaijan</b> due to&nbsp;<b>terrorism, armed conflict, </b>and<b> risk of landmines. </b>Some areas have increased risk. Read the entire Travel Advisory.<b></b></p>
<p><b>Do not travel to:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>The <b>southern border region</b> due to risk of <b>armed conflict (unless this is your best overland exit from Iran)</b>.</li>
<li>The <b>border with Armenia</b> due to risk of <b>landmines.</b></li>
<li>The following<b> territories</b> due to <b>landmines: </b>Aghdam, Aghdara, Fuzuli, Gubadli, Jabrayil, Kalbajar, Khankendi, Khojaly, Khojavend, Lach&#305;n, Shusha, and Zangilan</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Advisory Summary</b></p>
<p><b>Armed Conflict</b><br />
Following the onset of hostilities between the United States and Iran on February 28, 2026, there has been an ongoing threat of drone and missile attacks from Iran. There was a March 5, 2026, drone strike on Nakhchivan International Airport. Commercial flights have also been seriously disrupted.</p>
<p><b>Terrorism<br />
</b>There is risk of terrorist violence, including terrorist attacks and other activity in Azerbaijan. Visit the U.S. Department of State's <a href="https://www.state.gov/country-reports-on-terrorism/" target="_blank" title="DOS Country Reports on Terrorism">country reports on terrorism</a> to learn more.</p>
<p>Terrorist groups continue to plan attacks and are a risk in Azerbaijan. Terrorists may attack with little or no warning. They may target:</p>
<ul>
<li>Tourist locations</li>
<li>Transportation centers (airports)</li>
<li>Markets and shopping malls</li>
<li>Local government buildings</li>
<li>Hotels, clubs and restaurants</li>
<li>Places of worship</li>
<li>Parks</li>
<li>Major sporting and cultural events</li>
<li>Educational institutionsOther public areas</li>
<li>Other public areas</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Risks in specific areas<br />
Level 4 &#8211; Do not travel</b></p>
<p><b>Border with Iran<br />
Do not travel </b>to the<b> southern border region </b>due to risk of<b> armed conflict (unless this is your best overland exit from Iran)</b>.</p>
<p>Following the onset of hostilities between the United States and Iran on February 28, 2026, there has been an ongoing threat of drone and missile attacks from Iran. U.S. citizens should avoid the southern border area unless this is your best overland exit from Iran.</p>
<p><b>Border with Armenia<br />
Do not Travel </b>to the<b> border with Armenia </b>due to the risk of<b> landmines</b></p>
<p>Landmines and unexploded ordnance exist throughout this region. Many of them are not marked, so it can be hard to know where they are.</p>
<p><b>Exercise a high degree of caution on roads near the Azerbaijan-Armenia border</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Be aware that some portions of the road may cross international boundaries without notice.</li>
<li>Roads may be controlled by checkpoints or closed to travelers without notice.</li>
</ul>
<p>The U.S. government is unable to provide immediate emergency assistance to U.S. citizens because U.S. government employees working in Azerbaijan are prohibited from non-official travel to the border region.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Visit our website for <a href="https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/international-travel/before-you-go/travelers-with-special-considerations/high-risk-travelers.html" target="_blank" title="Travel to High-Risk Areas">Travel to High-Risk Areas.</a></p>
<p><b>Landmine-contaminated regions in Azerbaijan</b></p>
<ul>
<li><b>Do not travel to the following regions due to landmines: Aghdam, Aghdara, Fuzuli, Gubadli, Jabrayil, Kalbajar, Khankendi, Khojali, Khojavend, Lach&#305;n, Shusha, and Zangilan</b></li>
<li>Armed conflict has stopped in these regions, but landmines are still in the ground and remain dangerous.</li>
<li>Landmines and unexploded ordnance exist throughout this region. Many of them are not marked, so it can be hard to know where they are.</li>
<li>The U.S. government is unable to provide immediate emergency services to U.S. citizens in these regions because U.S. government employees need approval to travel there.</li>
</ul>

<p><b>If you decide to travel to Azerbaijan:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Enroll in the <a href="https://mytravel.state.gov/s/step" title="Smart Traveler Enrollment Program (STEP)">Smart Traveler Enrollment Program (STEP)</a> to get important updates and alerts from the U.S. embassy or consulate. Enrolling helps the U.S. embassy or consulate contact you or your emergency contact in an emergency.</li>
<li>Review the&nbsp;<a href="https://www.osac.gov/Content/Browse/Report?subContentTypes=Country%20Security%20Report" title="Country Security Report">Country Security Report</a>&nbsp;for Azerbaijan.</li>
<li>Have a plan to leave in an emergency that does not depend on U.S. government help. Review our information on <a href="https://travel.state.gov/en/international-travel/help-abroad/crisis-response.html" title="Crisis and Evacuations">Crisis and Evacuations</a>.</li>
<li>Review our information on <a href="https://travel.state.gov/en/international-travel/travel-advisories/global-events/terrorism.html" title="Terrorism">Terrorism</a> and <a href="https://travel.state.gov/en/international-travel/travel-advisories/high-risk-areas.html" title="Travel to High-Risk Areas">Travel to High-Risk Areas</a>.</li>
<li>Prepare a plan for emergency situations. Review the&nbsp;<a href="https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/international-travel/before-you-go/travelers-checklist.html" title="Traveler&#8217;s Checklist">Traveler&#8217;s Checklist</a>.</li>
<li>Visit the CDC page for the latest&nbsp;<a href="https://wwwnc.cdc.gov/travel/destinations/list" target="_blank" title="CDC Travel Health Information">Travel Health Information</a>&nbsp;related to your travel and return to the United States.</li>
<li>We highly recommend that you buy insurance before you travel. Check with your <a href="https://travel.state.gov/content/travel/en/international-travel/before-you-go/your-health-abroad/Insurance_Coverage_Overseas.html" title="Travel Insurance">travel insurance provider</a> about evacuation assistance, medical insurance, and trip cancelation coverage.</li>
</ul>
