The advisory was updated to reflect changes to U.S. embassy operations, warning against travel to Kuwait due to armed conflict, landmines, and crime. Key concerns include ongoing drone and missile attack threats following hostilities that began February 28, disrupted commercial aviation, and hazardous unexploded ordnance in desert and beach areas north of the Mutla'a Ridge.
