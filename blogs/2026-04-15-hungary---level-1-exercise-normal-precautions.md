Exercise normal precaution<p>in <b>Hungary</b>.</p>
<p><span class="body-regular">Hungary is generally a safe destination for travelers.</span></p>
<p><span class="body-regular">Most crimes against foreigners are crimes of opportunity, like purse snatching and pickpocketing, especially in tourist areas and major train stations in Budapest. This includes passport theft on the trains between Budapest, Vienna, and Bratislava.</span></p>
<ul>
<li><span class="body-regular">Review our travel guidance for Hungary to learn how to prepare for a safe trip.</span></li>
</ul>
